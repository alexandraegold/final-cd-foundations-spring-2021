ABOUT
Founded in 2021, Women in Mythology is a site intended to provide introductory information regarding the role and portrayal of women as beasts, mythical creatures, and more. It was created to fill an information gap for modern feminists exploring the historical portrayal of women in mythology. The links included throughout the text refer to various sources for deeper exploration.
